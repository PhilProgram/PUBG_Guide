Guide to PUBG: How to play

Step 1

Download Steam

Downloading Steam and PUBG

The first thing we need to do is download steam.

The first thing you need is a PC that can handle this game. This is a very graphics heavy game and it requires a few things.

  • 3 Cores at least
  • 980GTX Graphics card(I recommend 1060 6gb)
  • Good internet connection

These are just suggestions if you want to have a smooth game, the better the gear though the better the performance. If you didn't know already, Steam is required before you can play PUBG. Once you download Steam, go ahead and purchase a copy of PUBG. Now you have to download it, but don't worry: All of this is done within the Steam application so its safe, easy, and secure. Be patient - the game is very heavy at around 15GB so it might take up to an hour even.

Step 2

Choose from Solo, Squad, or Duo

Joining a match

Do training mode first, you will thank us later.

So now you have PUBG ready to launch, and you arrive at the main screen. Go ahead and click Play if you want, which will bring you to the lobby screen pictured here. However, we highly reccomend you enter training mode first. Make sure you check out the controls too. Getting used to the sensitivity is crucial so make sure youre mouse settings are adjusted. There are some great guides out there just for this.

Step 3: Playing

Sixty seconds till launch...

Get Ready

You have sixty seconds until launch.

If this is your first time, make sure you fly to a hot zone with a lot of people. You need combat experience at this point, and the best way is to drop at a crowded location. Make sure you stick with your team!

Gear up FAST

It is essential that you find a gun as fast as possible, reload, and check your surroundings.

Here we have an M-16 Assault rifle with a few magazines, as well as some handy hand grenades.

Be careful - kill as many people as you can. Check out more in the strategy section. Strategy

Once you have a gun and some gear, you can either play aggresively or camp. Eventually you will need to move, so in the early game you can stay put. But it might be a better idea to get a good position.

Step 4

Get to zone

Beware of the circle

There is a white circle that gets smaller and smaller - if you're outside, you will take damage that increases as the circle gets smaller

So you need to check your map often and plan out your next move. Get to the white circle zrea and you will be fine. The first few waves are fine. What this means as that while your in the blue during phase 1, the first circle decrease, the damage will be minimal. This holds true for the second phase as well. The fourth phase chunks baby, so be very careful! See my strategy for tips on how not to panic.

The end game

Winner Winner Chicken Dinner

If you are the last player standing, you win.

Interstengly enough, you can win without even taking a shot. It's happened to me, and it's not ultra rare. Let's say someone can't get it to circle in time after he's low health from a battle.
